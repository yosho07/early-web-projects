THE PARALYMPIC GAMES

☆WHAT ARE THE PARALYMPICS?☆

The Paralympic Games or Paralympics, also known as the Games of the Paralympiad, is a periodic series of international multi-sport events involving athletes with a range of disabilities, including impaired muscle power, impaired passive range of movement, limb deficiency, leg length difference, short stature, hypertonia, ataxia, athetosis, vision impairment and intellectual impairment.

PARALYMPICS
[Para·​lym·​pics]
: a series of international contests for athletes with disabilities that are associated with and held following the summer and winter Olympic Games.
